About Me

Turning Raw Data Into
Actionable Insights

I'm a Data Engineer who speaks both data and business. At Linxon, I work shoulder-to-shoulder with C-level executives — not just building pipelines, but translating strategic goals into data solutions that actually move the needle. From unifying scattered data sources into single-pane dashboards to automating processes that used to take days, I bridge the gap between what the business needs and what the data can deliver.

My toolkit spans the full Microsoft ecosystem — Power BI, Power Apps, Power Automate, Azure Data Factory, Synapse — combined with open-source heavy hitters like Spark, Kafka, and Airflow. Whether it's a batch warehouse or a real-time streaming pipeline, I design systems that are production-grade from day one: observable, scalable, and built with Infrastructure as Code.

Right now, I'm building something I'm especially proud of: a real-time global air quality monitoring platform on Azure — ingesting IoT sensor data from thousands of stations worldwide, running anomaly detection in Databricks, and triggering automated alerts when pollution crosses WHO thresholds. It's the kind of project that shows what data engineering looks like when it has a purpose beyond just moving bytes.
